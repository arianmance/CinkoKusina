C I N K O KUSINA

Five Women. One Kitchen. Endless Filipino Flavors.

Cinko Kusina is built by five passionate women who share the same love for modern Filipino cuisine. Our mission is to bring a fresh twist to the classic flavors we grew up with while staying true to the heart of Filipino cooking. Every dish we serve carries a story, a memory, and a taste of home – made with care, creativity and authenticity.

Our goal is to create a dining experience where tradition meets innovation. Whether it is a classic dish with a modern twist or a new creation inspired by Filipino culture, we take pride in offering food that brings comfort and joy to every customer.
